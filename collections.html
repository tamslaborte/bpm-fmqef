OUR COLLECTIONS

Our collections are gathered inside our Church, and Pre-Colonial Collections are gathered from the excavation site located outside of the Church. A unique characteristic of our collections is its rarity; you can only find them in our museum. Our collections are significant to the community because it can tell us what the town of Boljoon was before.
