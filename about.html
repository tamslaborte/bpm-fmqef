The Museum

This museum houses such religious relics as 1800 liturgical vestments and carved images of saints, it also displays the finding from archeological digs made just in front of the 18th century church.

Opened by Governor Gwendolyn Garcia, assisted by Mayor Deogenes Derama and Atty. Edmund N. Villanueva, president of the Boljoon Heritage Foundation Inc., on 11 November 2006 during celebrations marking the 407th town fiesta in honor of Nuestra Señora del Patrocinio de Maria.

General and Ecclesiastical Museum

Boljoon Parish Museum collects artifacts dated 18th century, 19th Century and 20th Century. We have also a Pre-Colonial Artifacts were dig in front of the Boljoon Church by National Museum and University of San Carlos.

Our museum focus on the history of the town and the church.
